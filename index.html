Date Format Help

Use the prefixes below to format your date. You can also add your own text or emojis to customize the display.

  • YYYY - Full year (e.g. 2025)
  • YY - Last two digits of year (e.g. 25)
  • MM - Full month name (e.g. June)
  • mm - Month number, zero-padded (e.g. 06)
  • M - Month abbreviation (e.g. Jun)
  • m - Month number (e.g. 6)
  • DD - Day number, zero-padded (e.g. 02)
  • D - Day number (e.g. 2)
  • Do - Day with ordinal (e.g. 2nd)
  • DDD - Day of the year (e.g. 154)
  • DDDo - Day of the year with ordinal (e.g. 154th)
  • W - Full weekday name (e.g. Monday)
  • w - Weekday abbreviation (e.g. Mon)
  • WW - ISO week number of the year (e.g. 23)
  • Q - Quarter number (e.g. 2)
  • Qo - Quarter with ordinal (e.g. 2nd)

Selecting a date template will clear the date format input and insert the corresponding prefix code for the chosen display.
