Date Format Help

Use the prefixes below to format your date. You can also add your own text or emojis to customize the display.

PrefixExample OutputDescription
YYYY2025Full year
YY25Last two digits of year
MMJulyFull month name
mm07Month number, zero-padded
MJulMonth abbreviation
m7Month number
DD04Day number, zero-padded
D4Day Number
Do4thDay with ordinal
DDD185Day of the year
DDDo185thDay of the year with ordinal
WFridayFull weekday name
wFriWeekday abbreviation
WW27ISO week number of the year
Q3Quarter number
Qo3rdQuarter with ordinal

Selecting a date template will clear the date format input and insert the corresponding prefix code for the chosen display.
